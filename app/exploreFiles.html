Always a pleasure scaffolding your apps.

With the process finished, let’s check out our new website! From cmd, type “grunt serve”. This will launch our website in your preferred browser. [img] Sure, this is great and all. But, what exactly did we make? Locate the folder where you specified installation. Yeoman stores the guts of your website in the /app/ folder. Your javascript file will be located in the /apps/scripts/ folder, and your css file is located in /apps/styles/. Next, let’s open up that index.html. [img] There’s a lot going on in there and it’s all been done for us thanks to yeoman! Without going in too deep on bootstrap, the design elements of the site are largely handed by bootstrap.
